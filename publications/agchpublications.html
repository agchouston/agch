AGC Houston Publications

AGC Houston is proud to provide our members with a suite of publications that keep the commercial construction community informed and connected. Through our partnership with m3 Magazines, one of the nation’s leading publishing firms, we deliver timely and relevant information through our biweekly Member’s Edge newsletter, quarterly Cornerstone magazine, and annual Membership Directory & Buyers’ Guide. These publications keep members up to date on everything happening at AGC Houston and the topics that matter most to our industry.

Cornerstone Magazine

AGC Houston publishes Cornerstone, a quarterly magazine dedicated to providing members with relevant information about the Chapter, its members, and issues impacting the commercial construction industry.

In 2025, Cornerstone was fully reimagined and redesigned to better serve our readers. Earlier this year, AGC Houston partnered with m3 Magazines, giving our publication a fresh look and the opportunity to deliver even more industry-focused content.

This redesign has been recognized with a nomination for the 2025 Folio Eddie & Ozzie Award for Best Redesign, highlighting the exceptional work our friends at m3 Magazines have done to elevate our publications.

The Associated General Contractors of America, Inc. Houston Chapter (AGC Houston) is a local construction trade association that represents and provides services to commercial building general contractors, subcontractors, suppliers, manufacturers and service firms. Founded in 1923, Houston AGC is one of 89 chapters nationwide.
