Member Committees

AGC Houston offers its members a variety of ways to get involved in chapter activities. Joining a committee provides members with great networking opportunities as well supporting the membership and industry by sharing their talents and expertise.

Make the most of your membership and get involved today! Contact the staff liaison associated with the specific committee.

The Associated General Contractors of America, Inc. Houston Chapter (AGC Houston) is a local construction trade association that represents and provides services to commercial building general contractors, subcontractors, suppliers, manufacturers and service firms. Founded in 1923, Houston AGC is one of 89 chapters nationwide.
